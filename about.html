Our Story

A bakery built on patience and butter.

Mphoentle Bakery started in a tiny home kitchen with a single oven and a great-grandmother's sourdough starter. Today, we're a neighborhood landmark — but the recipe hasn't changed: real ingredients, slow time, and care in every fold.

Every morning before sunrise, our bakers shape, score, and bake by hand. We mill local flour, use grass-fed butter, and bake in small batches so every loaf comes out singing.
